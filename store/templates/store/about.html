About

The Embedded Store is a Full Stack Web Development project undertaken by Job Jacob, Leo Varghese and Mohammed Zeeshan, all BTech undergraduate students at Federal Institute of Science and Technology (FISAT). The aim of this project is to develop a fully functional E-commerce website for buying project related hardware components by anyone around the world.

For more information on this project, please be free to contact us.
